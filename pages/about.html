About

I'm an interdisciplinary designer based in London with over 10 years experience working either in-house, freelance or agency-side (digital or print). Over the years, I have collaborated with a wide range of individuals and organisations — from the very small to the exceedingly large — across a broad swathe of sectors, from education and consumer finance through to logistics and the charity sector.

Right now, I am is currently as an in-house Designer at TotallyMoney.com with responsibiliies across their technology and creative teams. My duties currently include contributing to our in-house design language, architecting CSS and HTML, providing a steady stream of visual designs for up-coming product releases, and supporting as well as mentoring colleagues where needed.

I prefer coffee over tea, am an avid reader/writer, badminton player, and multiple-hat-wearing gent who occasionally likes to roll twenties and crit dragons in my sleep.

PS: I am available to hire for small freelance projects for a terribly immodest fee.
