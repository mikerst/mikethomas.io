Thoughts and opinions

  • Designers should absolutely 100% be involved in writing the code-that-corresponds-to-realising-their-designs. If this isn't happening, re-think your team.
  • The front-endification of design is a good thing. But with great power, comes great responsibility!
  • No process > too much process.
  • Process and Method are not the same thing (good recurring processes facilitate a good methodology).
  • Common sense and intuition are just as valuable in making decisions as hard data.
  • Your users should always come first, but they are not always right.
  • Better Call Saul is a better show than Breaking Bad. Yep.
  • It is ok to say you're 'going to the bathroom' even if no bath is present in that room.
