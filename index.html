About

Multi-disciplinary designer, with a love of great coffee, old book smells, looking out for others, and perfect dropshots.

I've just completed a six year stint at TotallyMoney, a FinTech scale-up near Silicon Roundabout, where I've worked across Brand, Marketing and Product teams, in an always-interesting, ever-evolving role.

Throughout this time, and prior, I've had the good fortune to work with clients and organisations such as the NHS, DB Schenker, Cognita Schools, Capital One, MBNA, Barclaycard, Southbank International School and many, many more.

Right now I'm taking a short break, and looking for new opportunities to commence in the spring. Portfolio available on request.
